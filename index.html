    About Us

    We are an environmental consultancy firm specializing in environmental impact assessment (EIA), public hearing and seeking No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the designated EPA(Environmental Protection Agency) for our client's project.

    Our Mission

    We provide efficient consultation which complies with strict environmental regulations.We are an environmental consultancy firm specializing in environmental impact assessment (EIA), public hearing and seeking No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the designated EPA(Environmental Protection Agency) for our client's project

    Our Plan

    To promote sustainable development within the confines of standards set by World Bank and Environmental Protection Agency consequently, mitigating the adverse effects of extreme industrialization on our environment

    Our Vision

    We want to do our part in promoting greener future for the prosperity of our industrial sector and our nation.

    Services

    We excel in providing our clientage the best consultation services. Some of them are mentioned as following.

    Environmental Compliance

    We excel in conforming to environmental laws, regulations, standards and other requirements such as site permits to operate

    NOC From Environmental Protection Agency

    We provide valuable consultation in getting NOC from Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), which is environmental regulatory body of Pakistan.

    Environmental Impact Assessment Report

    We provide integral Environmental Impact Assessment Report which is a structured process for considering the implications, for people and their environment, of proposed actions while there is still an opportunity to modify (or even, if appropriate, abandon) the proposals.

    Sustainable Environmental Solutions

    Sustainable Environmental Solutions offers a range of services for carrying out Consultancy, design and construction supervision services in the areas of Health, Environment, Renewable Energy and Industrial Safety.

    National Environmental quality standards(NEQS) and PEQS

    SES also offers complete solutions in the area of water and wastewater on baseline study, designing, and construction in the light of national Environmental quality standards(NEQS) and PEQS

    World Bank Group Guidelines and Equator principal

    Environmental studies for new projects in the light of Pakistan environmental legislation, World Bank Group Guidelines and Equator principal

    Auditing Reports

    Environmental auditing Reports for existing plants

    Compliance Reports

    Compliance reports of existing plants

    Training Programs

    Training on environment , health and safety

    Legal Solicitations

    Legal soliciting on issues relating to environmental legislation
